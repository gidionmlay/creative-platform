FAQ

Frequency & Questions

What services does G Design offer?

G Design offers a comprehensive suite of creative services including our online academy with industry-leading courses, intensive bootcamps for career transformation, professional production studio services for film and music projects, and creative commerce solutions to help African talent monetize their skills.

How long does it take to complete a bootcamp?

Our bootcamps range from 8-12 weeks depending on the specialization. Each program includes intensive hands-on training, real-world projects, and career support. We also offer flexible part-time options and self-paced online courses for those who need more time.

Do you offer ongoing support after graduation?

Absolutely! G Design provides lifetime access to our alumni network, career counseling, portfolio reviews, and job placement assistance. Our graduates also get priority access to advanced courses and networking events with industry professionals.

Are your courses recognized by industry employers?

Yes! Our programs are designed in partnership with leading creative companies and our graduates work at top studios, agencies, and production houses across Africa and globally. We provide industry-recognized certifications and help build portfolios that get noticed.

What is your pricing model for courses and services?

We offer flexible pricing options including full scholarships for exceptional talent, payment plans for bootcamps, and affordable online course subscriptions. Our production studio services are competitively priced with packages designed to fit different project budgets and creative needs.
